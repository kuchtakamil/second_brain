Możesz powiedzieć to krótko i zwięźle na przykład tak:

Jasne! Oto kilka wersji po angielsku, w zależności od stylu:

### 1. **Neutralna, zwięzła:**
> I'm a software engineer with over 10 years of experience, mainly working with Java and Erlang. I've been working remotely for the past 6 months.

### 2. **Trochę bardziej swobodna:**
> I've been a software engineer for over 10 years, mostly working with Java and Erlang. For the past 6 months, I've been working from home.

### 3. **Bardziej formalna:**
> I have more than 10 years of experience as a software engineer, primarily focused on Java and Erlang. For the last 6 months, I’ve been working in a remote setup.
