**Gemini:**

**eatable** – acceptable or pleasant to eat; has an okay or good taste.\
**edible** – safe to consume; won't harm you if eaten (not poisonous).\
**uneatable** – not fit to be eaten because it tastes bad, is overcooked, or is unpleasant.\
**inedible** – not fit to be eaten because it is poisonous, indigestible, or not meant for consumption.

---

**drinkable** – acceptable or pleasant to drink; has an okay or good taste.\
**potable** – safe to drink; meets health and safety standards (not contaminated).\
**undrinkable** – not fit to be drunk because it tastes bad, is flat, or is unpleasant.\
**non-potable** – not safe to drink; contains harmful toxins, chemicals, or bacteria.
