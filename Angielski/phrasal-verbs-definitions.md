**Assistant:**

**Take off** – remove something from the container or separate the lid from it.  
*You take off the lid of the container.*

**Take out** – remove an object from inside the container.  
*You take out the item from the container.*

**Put down** – place the container or the object onto a surface.  
*You put down the container on the table.*

**Pick up** – lift the container or the object from a surface.  
*You pick up the container from the shelf.*

**Put in** – place an object inside the container.  
*You put in the item and close the container.*

**Put on** – place the lid onto the container so it’s covered.  
*You put on the lid to close the container.*
