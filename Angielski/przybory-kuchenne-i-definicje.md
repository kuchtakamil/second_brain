### Lista z definicjami

rolling pin – A rolling pin is a cylindrical kitchen tool used to flatten and shape dough evenly by rolling it over a surface.  

meat mallet – A meat mallet is a heavy tool used to pound meat in order to make it thinner and more tender.  

strainer – A strainer is a kitchen utensil with a fine mesh used to separate liquid from small solid food particles.  

colander – A colander is a bowl-shaped container with holes, used to drain water from foods like pasta or vegetables.  

---

### Dodatkowe przybory kuchenne

cutting board – A cutting board is a flat surface used to safely cut, chop, or slice food during preparation.  

chef’s knife – A chef’s knife is a large, versatile knife used for chopping, slicing, and cutting many types of food.  

spatula – A spatula is a flat tool used to flip, lift, or spread food while cooking or baking.  

whisk – A whisk is a tool made of wire loops used to mix ingredients and add air to liquids like eggs or cream.  

ladle – A ladle is a deep spoon with a long handle, used to serve soups, sauces, or stews.  

peeler – A peeler is a small handheld tool used to remove the skin from fruits and vegetables.  

grater – A grater is a tool with sharp holes used to shred foods such as cheese, vegetables, or zest.  

measuring cup – A measuring cup is a container marked with units used to measure liquid or dry ingredients accurately.
