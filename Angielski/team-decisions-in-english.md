**Assistant:**

Jasne! Oto kilka naturalnych sposobów wyrażenia tych myśli po angielsku:

### 1. **"Coś musimy ustalić w zespole"**
- **"We need to align on this as a team."** – bardzo często używane w pracy, szczególnie w kontekście zespołów Agile.
- **"We need to agree on this within the team."**
- **"This is something we still need to decide as a team."**
- **"Let's discuss this and reach a consensus in the team."** – bardziej formalne.

### 2. **"Coś zostało ustalone i wygląda to tak..."**
- **"We've agreed on the following..."**
- **"Here's what we’ve decided as a team..."**
- **"The team has aligned on this, and it looks like this..."**
- **"This is the outcome of our discussion/decision..."**
